Eco Friendly Packaging

Stop throwing plastic out, we recycle all of our packaging and offer a return-and-reuse program to minimise landfill

No SLS, SLES or Parabens

We use all natural ingredients in the making of our soap, although it's a more complicated process to build a natural product, we truely believe in selling a product we use at home.

No Animal Products

Animal products (Sodium tallowate) is used in almost all commercial soaps, it's derived from beef or sheep and is often used as a cheap substitute to vegetable fats. We only use vegetable derived ingrediants.

Great for the Enviornment

We do everything we can to produce a green world. We source our ingredients locally, reusable packaging, and we even have solar on the roof.
